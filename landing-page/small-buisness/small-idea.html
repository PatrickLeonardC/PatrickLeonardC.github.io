Small-Business Idea

About

ACID apparel is a mock-up idea of a clothing company, which would market to younger generations. It would focus on keeping up with trends by having an AI track and monitor market data of popular items off tiktok, or other social media platforms. and then put it into a spreadsheet of ideas which would then allow workers at the company to design and produce new products ensuring that the company does not fall behind in the latest trends or designs.

Budget

The budget is all fictional however the start-up cost would be above average compared to a regular clothing start-up. However the idea behind the AI ensures that there would not need to be as many fashion designers, and other kinds of employees, saving money in the long run. Less workers = more money
